Linked Satellites
Automatically listed from linked moons and orbital stations.

For planets and Waystations, orbit distance/speed controls spacing and movement around Osiris. For moons and other linked satellites, it controls spacing and movement around the parent body. Names are locked for all planets, Rantel Waystations, Osiris, and the Rantel Cluster. Faction-control overrides are available for every celestial body and must total exactly 100% when enabled. Moon textures can be replaced from this editor. The asteroid-density slider appears only when the Rantel Cluster is selected.

Core system bodies are protected. Select a linked moon, Waystation, or satellite to delete it and its attached campaign data.
